"Tired of losing client details in your Gmail inbox?"

Trade Mail Trio extracts key info on demand, reminds you of promises, and helps you reply professionally in the right tone — built for cross-border business.

What It Does

📝

Email Summaries

One-click TL;DRs and key points. Prices, dates, ports, Incoterms, certifications, and HS codes are automatically highlighted.

📋

Action Extraction

AI scans for promises, requests, and deadlines. Items categorized as Client Requests, Your Promises, Client Commitments, or Needs Reply.

✍️

Draft Polishing

Rewrite drafts in 4 styles: Professional, Concise, Friendly, or Assertive. Cross-border trade terminology is built in.

👥

Client CRM

Every client's history — summaries, pending actions, resolved items — grouped in one place. Collapsible year/month/day folders.

🤝

Work Handoff

Double-layer password protection. Export client data as an AES-256-GCM encrypted .tmt file. Your colleague imports with the password you share — all action items and summaries transfer securely.

🔮

Coming Soon: Custom Terminology

Upload your product SKUs, internal codes, and domain jargon. TMT learns your business vocabulary for precision-matched AI responses.
